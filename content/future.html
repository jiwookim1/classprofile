High School Rating

72.2% of students gave the SIS high school experience a 7 out of 10 or more. Whether it was the quality of education or friends that supported their journey, SIS students had an overall positive experience at the school.

While students still have numerous complaints about the school, this proves that other factors are more important to having a positive experience. School is what you make of it.
