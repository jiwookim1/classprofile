Family and Relationships

Family

All of us spent time with our family each week. The average amount of time spent in a week with parents was 23.4 hours or 3.3 hours per day.

average amount of time (hours) spent with parents in a week

A good chunk of us will separate from our parents when we graduate. Around 14% will stay, and 22% are unsure if they will.. or won't.
