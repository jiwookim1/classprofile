Lifestyle and Experiences

Sleep

Sleep Rules Everything Around Me. The most popular answer was 12 AM, with a third of the students choosing that option. However, many students preferred sleeping at a later time, with half of all respondents choosing to sleep between 1am to 3am.

Everyone went to sleep by 3am.

usual sleep time

Some students valued sleep more than others. Only 19% of respondents slept >= 8 hours on average, which is the recommended amount for teenagers by the CDC. For those who missed out on sleep for school, now is the time to get it back!
