About the School

Singapore International School @ Gamuda Gardens is a modern, well-maintained international school campus located in Hoang Mai District, Hanoi. Accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC), SIS@GG provides a well-rounded education from Pre-Nursary to Year 12 students (THAT'S US!)

Throughout high school, the graduating class of 2022 successfully completed 8 semesters or 16 terms of school, writing the IGCSE, AS and A Level exams provided by Cambridge Assessment International Education. This profile attempts to showcase who SIS @ GG 2022 students are, what they've done, and how they experienced high school.

This profile is not affiliated with Singapore International School or KinderWorld International Group in any way.

Preface

The findings in this report were derived from the results of a class survey done through Google Forms. In total, 36 out of 37 (97%) graduating students responded.

Being the largest graduating class of SIS ever, this year's class may have gone through a different experience than past graduating classes. Experiences will vary significantly from year to year.

Study terms are defined as IG1, IG2, AS, and AL. Government shutdowns due to Covid-19 began in Febuary 2020, halfway through IG2. IG2 exams were cancelled due to it, and all students received a predicted grade which became their final grades for their subjects. Read more here on what happened.

Although we went back to school in the beginning of AS Level, schools closed down again 1 day before the Cambridge Exams began. Exams were still allowed to take place, and most students took the AS Level exam in-person. Due to prolonged shutdowns, most of our A Level learnings took place online, which inevitably reduced the quality of learning, our motivation, and opportunities to thrive as a Senior. We finally went back to school in Febuary 2022, and wrote our final A Level papers.
